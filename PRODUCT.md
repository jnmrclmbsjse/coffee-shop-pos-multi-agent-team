# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Coffee-shop administrators and staff use this internal application at the
counter and in back-office workflows. They need to complete point-of-sale,
catalog, inventory-counting, and operational tasks quickly and confidently,
with administrator-only areas clearly protected.

## Product Purpose

UCM Coffee Studio's application provides one dependable workspace for daily
shop operations. It should keep routine work efficient, protect sensitive
administrative functions, and remain ready for a second branch without
introducing workflows that do not belong in the current product scope.

## Brand Personality

Calm, trustworthy, and precise. The interface should feel operational and
approachable, with direct language and enough warmth to belong to the studio
without distracting from the task.

## Anti-references

Avoid consumer-marketing theatrics, decorative coffee-shop clichés, glossy
dashboard decoration, novelty controls, and dense enterprise administration
screens. Protected content must never be used as decoration or previewed before
authorization.

## Design Principles

- Put the active operational task first and keep supporting context concise.
- Prefer familiar, consistent controls that disappear into the workflow.
- Make permissions, errors, and system state clear without exposing sensitive
  account details.
- Preserve the user's place across transitions such as authentication and page
  reloads.
- Keep the interface responsive and dependable on counter and back-office
  devices.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Support keyboard-only operation, explicit labels,
programmatically associated validation messages, visible focus, minimum 44px
primary touch targets, sufficient color contrast, polite live announcements,
and reduced-motion preferences.
