# Product

## Register

product

## Users

UCM Coffee Studio staff and administrators at a single active shop, with a second branch expected soon. Administrators maintain the catalog, category order, products, sizes, prices, mappings, and availability. Counter staff primarily take orders and temporarily mark products available or sold out.

## Product Purpose

Provide one dependable operational reference for maintaining the shop catalog and reflecting temporary availability in the POS. Success means staff can distinguish catalog configuration from today’s service availability, make valid changes without ambiguity, and understand when those changes become visible elsewhere.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, operational. The interface should feel trustworthy during a busy shift and efficient during catalog maintenance.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic admin templates, oversized dashboard summaries, decorative imagery, excessive cards, gradients, novelty controls, and consumer-commerce styling. Do not imply offline operation, hardware integration, bill-of-material depletion, inter-branch logistics, or real-time stock ledgers.

## Design Principles

1. Put the current task and its consequences in view.
2. Separate long-lived catalog state from temporary service availability.
3. Make authoritative ordering tactile and explicit.
4. Prevent invalid or destructive changes with specific, local explanations.
5. Prefer familiar, keyboard-accessible controls over custom interaction novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and interaction behavior. Maintain visible labels, keyboard focus, 44px minimum targets, non-color state cues, reduced-motion support, readable validation, and responsive layouts without horizontal page scrolling.
