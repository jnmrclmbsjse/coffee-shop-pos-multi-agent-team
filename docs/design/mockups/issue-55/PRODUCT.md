# UCM Coffee Studio Back Office

## Register

product

## Users

UCM Coffee Studio owners and administrators managing inventory setup from a bright back office or the service counter during a busy shift. They need to find, configure, and protect operational records quickly while handling interruptions.

## Product Purpose

Issue #55 adds administrative management for stock categories, stock items, count methods, reconciliation rules, and independent normal-day and peak-day par levels. Success means an administrator can create and maintain valid inventory configuration, understand why a destructive or invalid action is blocked, and recover without losing saved values.

This surface configures inventory records. It does not display live on-hand quantities or imply automatic depletion.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, operational. The product should feel dependable under time pressure, use direct language, and make state changes easy to verify.

## Anti-references

- Marketing dashboards with oversized metrics, promotional copy, or decorative visualization.
- Novelty admin controls, drag-only ordering, hidden labels, or interaction patterns that require a tutorial.
- Decorative gradients, glass effects, illustrated empty states, excessive card nesting, and color-only status signals.
- Interfaces that imply live stock, automatic depletion, or unsupported operational workflows.

## Design Principles

1. Put the current task and its consequences in view.
2. Prefer familiar controls and explicit state over clever interactions.
3. Keep saved data safe when validation or server rules reject a change.
4. Explain recovery beside the problem, then summarize it at save time.
5. Preserve operational context across desktop, tablet, and mobile layouts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Use visible labels and focus indicators, semantic keyboard-friendly controls, minimum 44px touch targets, non-color status cues, clear error associations, and reduced-motion support. Responsive layouts must avoid page-level horizontal scrolling and convert dense tables into labeled rows on narrow screens.

## Explicit Non-goals

- Offline operation
- Hardware integration
- Recipe or bill-of-materials depletion
- Inter-branch logistics
- Real-time stock ledger
- Stock-count workflow
